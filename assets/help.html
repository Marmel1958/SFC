Help with this app

This app allows your visit to the events of Wittenberg hard to plan "Luther's Wedding".

The app lists all events (Source: Wittenberg city marketing).You can select venues. From a distance of about 10 km, there is the possibility to choose the next venue of their current location automatically. The app also includes a map indicating the venues (and possibly their location). Venues can be specifically selected on the map.Planning a route from their current Location to your selected venue is ife possible.

Events in the past are red, shown in black in the future. Currently running events are shown in green.

A click on the corresponding entry opens a window with details. A long click opens a menu with more options, such as entry into their calendar or parts of the event via Twitter or email. The graduation options depend on the apps installed on your device.





Enjoy your celebrations!
